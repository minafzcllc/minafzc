Who We Are

A UAE-based partner for dental, surgical and medical instruments

MINA FZC LLC is a UAE-based manufacturer, supplier, wholesaler and exporter of dental, surgical and medical instruments. From our base in Ajman, United Arab Emirates, we serve distributors, hospitals, dental clinics, medical centers and healthcare procurement teams with instruments built from medical-grade stainless steel — precision engineered, corrosion resistant and fully autoclavable.

Every instrument that leaves our facility is inspected for finish, alignment and function, because the professionals who use our tools work where accuracy is non-negotiable. Whether you are stocking a single clinic or supplying a national distribution network, we deliver consistent quality, wholesale pricing and dependable export logistics.

Quality & Sterilization

Medical-grade, batch after batch

Quality in surgical instruments is not a finish — it is a process. Each instrument passes through material selection, forming, precision grinding, finishing and final inspection before it is packed for export.

Medical Grade Stainless Steel

Instruments are produced from medical-grade stainless steel selected for hardness, edge retention and biocompatibility in clinical use.

Precision Engineering

Working ends are ground and finished to fine tolerances, then checked for alignment, symmetry and smooth action.

Autoclave-Safe by Design

Every instrument is designed for standard steam autoclave cycles — no coatings or joints that degrade under repeated sterilization.

Corrosion Resistance

Passivated, corrosion-resistant surfaces withstand disinfectants, ultrasonic cleaning and humid clinical environments.

Final Inspection

Visual and functional inspection of finish, tips and mechanisms before packing, with batch-level consistency across wholesale orders.

Documentation Support

Product and export documentation assistance for institutional buyers, importers and regulatory submissions.

Care guidance: clean promptly after use, sterilize per your facility protocol, and store dry.
